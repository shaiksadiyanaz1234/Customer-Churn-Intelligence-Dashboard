# 📊 Customer Churn Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed to analyze customer churn behavior and identify key factors influencing customer retention.

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor customer churn, evaluate contract performance, analyze customer segments, and support data-driven retention strategies.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and long-term business growth. The objective of this project is to identify customers at high risk of churn and uncover actionable insights to improve customer retention.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze customer churn trends
- Build executive KPI dashboard
- Identify churn drivers
- Evaluate customer segments
- Provide business recommendations
- Support data-driven decision making

## Business Insights

- Month-to-month customers have the highest churn.
- Electronic check users churn more frequently.
- Fiber optic customers show higher churn rates.
- Early tenure customers are at greater risk.
- Long-term contracts improve retention.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Improve onboarding for new customers.
- Promote long-term contracts.
- Encourage automatic payment methods.
- Improve customer experience for high-risk segments.
- Launch proactive customer retention campaigns.
